ABOUT

PHANTASMAGORICS is a design studio. We are focused on Interiors+ : Full-scale interior renovations, site-specific interior installations, and the finishing and furnishing of interiors. We also design outdoor environments of linked interior spaces.

Our work is user-tailored, narrative-driven, heritage-informed, and easefully modern. We:

Reshape and reconfigure space.

Employ engaging textures and alluring materials.

Implement clever color and creative lighting.

Source and design captivating objects and furnishings.

Structured via schematic and design development, construction documentation and administration – we create spaces that are artful, sometimes droll, always transformative, and entirely fulling.

We are based in New York City.



Jessica Salomons-Bordes is a designer and the founder of PHANTASMAGORICS.

Jessica graduated from The New School – Parsons School of Design in NYC with an AAS in Interior Design. She earned a BS in Business Administration from Babson College in Wellesley, Massachusetts.

Pivoting to design in 2017, Jessica worked with small New York City based architectural studios before dedicating herself full-time to PHANTASMAGORICS in 2022. She previously was a private equity fundraiser, first in Paris, France then in New York City.

Jessica is a proud member of Female Design Council.
